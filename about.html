About This Site

I feel a sense of discomfort. Many web designers claim to create minimalistic websites, yet their reliance on globally reused templates strikes me as absurd.Don’t you think so? They pack their sites with cookies, rely on external fonts, depend heavily on Google services, and while the surface may appear minimal, these web pages are built in a way that won’t even last ten years, let alone five.

It’s reminiscent of activists who preach sustainability on the global stage but fly around the world in private jets funded by money taken from the poor.

That’s why I set out to create the opposite: a website that could still be accessed 50 years from now. It doesn’t rely on libraries, nor does it extract any of your data. This makes the site lightning-fast.

GDPR? Such a trivial framework has nothing to do with this site. After all, I’m not taking anything from you—except a bit of your time.

Here’s my belief: if one aims for minimalism, they should strive for true minimalism. Similarly, conceptual artists, above all, must remain unwaveringly loyal to their concepts.

The total size of this website, at about 550KB, is equivalent to just 0.2 seconds of streaming a YouTube video in HD quality.

This is why this homepage exists, not just as a website but as a singular work of art by me, an artist. If this philosophy resonates with you, I kindly ask for a $1 contribution to help cover the domain and server costs.

Your support ensures this site remains online and accessible for the next 50 years.
