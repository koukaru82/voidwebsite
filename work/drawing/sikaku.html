Sikaku

Sikaku means rectangle.I painted this with the thought that it's fascinating how the human unconscious fills an otherwise empty space.

When I reflect on it, it reminds me of the *hishi-mochi* eaten on celebratory days for young girls in Japan.

Acrylic gouache.
